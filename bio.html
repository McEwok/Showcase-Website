Bio

I have loved music all my life. I grew up with music in my home. I grew up with a mom that listened to country music and a dad that listened to jazz and easy listening music. To say the least I was exopsed to two different sides of the musical spectrum. In fifth grade, I started to learn the violin. I learned to love classical music. By eighth grade, I discovered rap music. I spent hours drawing graffiti and listening to Bone Thugz and Harmony. At eighteen I started living the hip hop life and spent many years doing so. I pretty much wasted ten years of my life. It took me another two years after my divorce to get my life back together. While dating my wife, she introduced me K Love Radio. It plays all christian music. I learned how to do music production down in California when I lived there. I also know how to rap and write rhymes. I decided to create gospel rap. My goal is to get the message out about our Savior Jesus Christ.
